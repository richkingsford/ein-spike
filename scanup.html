How detection works (prototype)

This prototype simulates a 2-stage detector: a proposer lists candidate components with confidence scores, and you confirm or reject each one. Low-confidence proposals are surfaced for review — that's the lever that drives both false positives and false negatives toward zero. In production, the proposer would be a vision-language model and the verifier would re-check each candidate against the cropped region.
